What is happening in your head/mind/brain when you add numbers?

We can all describe it to a certain extent, any maybe that's what went on it Wundt's laboratory. But those descritions are essentially subjective and not behaviourist - can we examine the 'behaviour'?
Yes, how long does it take to add 2 numbers and how does that time compare to adding 3 or 4 or 11?
Is it the same time taken for how ever many digits? Or does the number of digits matter?
Is there a pattern? Even a correlation?
And once we have examined our results what can we now 'infer' about the 'mind'?
