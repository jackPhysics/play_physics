Working memory test for numeric figures


INSTRUCTIONS:


Press ‘GO!’ button.


The word “Start” is shown on the screen for 2 s.


13 double digit numbers are shown, one at a time, at the centre of the screen, at the speed of 2 s per number.


If you see a number that has ALREADY APPEARED BEFORE press the ‘Yes’ button.


The answer is then displayed showing:


  • correct: how many numbers you spotted correctly

  • wrong: how many numbers you incorrectly thought had been repeated


and then a list of the 13 numbers colour-coded and number-coded to show how you did


coding:


    • black - 0=no repeat – number not in the 18

    • pink - 1=repeat missed – number in the 18 missed

    • green - 2=repeat found – number in the 18 found

    • red - 3=false positive – number not in the 18 incorrectly identified
