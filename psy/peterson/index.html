How long can you remember something for?

After you press "GO!" a 3 letter TRIGRAM will be displayed.
- the trigram will then disappear and a number will appear
- you must SPEAK OUT LOUD counting down the number displayed at the rate of 1 number per second
- every 3 seconds the number will be updated to keep you on track in your countdown
- after 3, 9 or 18 seconds an input box will appear for you to input the original trigram
