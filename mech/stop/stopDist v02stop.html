STOP when it says 'STOP!'

STOP the car by clicking anyway on the scene.

To go again press the 'restart' button

When the simulation starts the car is already moving. You will then need to click on the scene when told to 'stop' - depending on which simulation you choose there are increasingly harder (they require greater attention) criteria for when you must stop.

You might have to wait up to 60 seconds - do not lose focus!

Clicking to stop before you are told to, will incure a time penalty added to your reaction time!

Sims can be 'paused' by pressing the "STOP" button - "restart" button restarts the clock.

The simulation will keep a record of all your reaction times - to reset this 'refresh' (F5) the page.
