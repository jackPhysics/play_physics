Protons are RED. Neutrons are BLUE. You have 50 of each.

(if keys not working click on the viewing area to activate them) Z/Q = Move nucleon with the focus IN/OUT
cursorLEFT/cursorRIGHT = rotate all LEFT/RIGHT
cursorUP/cursorDOWN = rotate all UP/DOWN
N/M = rotate all LEFT/RIGHT
O/L = rotate all UP/DOWN
S = Show which nucleon has the focus
J = Jump the focus to another nucleon
F = Flip the nucleon with the focus to the opposite type - n to p, p to n

The small black dot represents the centre of mass of the whole system (just its xy coords, rotate image for depth information).
