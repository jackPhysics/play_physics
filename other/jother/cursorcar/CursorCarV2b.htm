If car stalls, eg it doesn't respond to the keys, try clicking in the middle to restart the engine.

You have three modes to choose from: acceleration, speed or distance.

The mode tells you what changes when you press the forward (or reverse cursor key).
For example if you are in distance mode then pressing the forward key changes the distance of the car - it moves! Letting go of the key stops changing the distance - it stops!
If you are in speed mode then pressing the forward key changes the speed of the car. The speed then changes the distance (because that is what 'speed' is - 'change of distance'). And when you stop pressing the key the speed stops changing - this does not mean the speed goes to zero! The speed stays constant, it does not change! - try it out to see.
If you are in acceleration mode then pressing the forward key changes the acceleration of the car. The acceleration then continuously changes the speed (because that is what 'acceleration' is - 'change of speed'). It's like having the gas pedal stuck down! Oops! Try it out.

The LEFT and RIGHT cursor keys are used for steering

You can quickly change between acceleration, speed and distance by using the A, S and D keys on the keyboard (note that when you do the acceleration and velocity of the car are reset to 0).
