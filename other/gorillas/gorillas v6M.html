To start a match refresh the page (F5)
Each player takes turn throwing a banana
- TO SHOOT: click on screen - the point of click gives ANGLE and SPEED
angle is between 0 and 89 degrees
speed is proportional to distance between click point and gorilla
For each match the gravity is slightly different,
(somewhere between g/4 to 2g) but stays the same for the whole match (until you refresh the page)
Play until a certain number of hits (eg 1, 3, 5, ...)
Or to a certain number of shots (eg 10, 12, 15,...)
and whoever has the higher number of hits, or points, wins
'points' measure how many shots it took to get a hit - more shots, more points.
